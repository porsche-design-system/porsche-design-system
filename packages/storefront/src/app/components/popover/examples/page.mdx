## Controlled

By default the `p-popover`component has a fully managed internal state and manages its own open/close state. But the
component can also be used with a **slotted button** as a controlled component. This means that the behavior must be
controlled from outside by using the `open` prop. Don't forget to also set the `aria-expanded` and focus state by your
own.
